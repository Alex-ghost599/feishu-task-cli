# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming project. Be respectful, discuss technical
claims with evidence, and protect private data. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment,
discrimination, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, and abuse of project
infrastructure.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit,
remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
needed to protect the community.
